# Technical Architect Charter Context

This context defines the TA function's scope. All insights and recommendations MUST stay within these boundaries.

## Mission

Technical Architects help GTM win **bigger**, close **faster**, and set up our most strategic customers for **success and growth**.

## Three Pillars

- **Integrations** — Embed SafetyCulture into customer ecosystems (ERP, Workforce Management, legacy systems)
- **Identity** — Architect scalable enterprise access and provisioning
- **Information** — Ensure SafetyCulture high-value data reaches those who matter

## Engagement Criteria

- New and expansion opportunities
- $100k+ AUD and/or Highly Strategic (approved by TA Leader or regional MD/GM)
- Must satisfy 2 of: Strong Compelling Event, Complex Integrations, Highly Regulated, Competitive RFP/RFI

## What TAs Do

- Technical Discovery — Structured investigation of requirements, constraints, systems, and risks
- Map pain to technology — Translate business challenges into targeted technical capabilities and solutions
- Show art of the possible — Build custom automations to show complex integrations
- Solution Architecture — Design scalable, secure, aligned systems to meet requirements
- Build trust with IT leaders — Engage with prospect IT leaders to increase buyer confidence
- Derisk opportunities — Identify and mitigate technical and delivery risks during the sales process
- Handoff to post-sales teams — Transfer validated solution context, architecture, and risks

## What TAs Do NOT Do

- Solution Delivery (that's COMs and CSEs)
- Technical Support (that's the support team)
- Talk Pricing (owned by AEs and their leadership)
- Product Management / feature requests
- Standard Demos (done by field teams)
- Project Management (TAs are consultative account partners focused on winning Enterprise deals)
- Overpromise (no roadmap items, custom features, or known problematic integrations)

## Exit Criteria

- Solution Architecture Overview (SAO) — required for complex deals
- Documentation in Salesforce — technical details captured in Chatter at opportunity level
- Technical Validation from prospect — customer technical approval and preferred solution status
